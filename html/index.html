Welcome

Welcome to the Colbert County Community Development Commission's Website. The CCCDC uses TVA in lieu of taxes money to fund grants to local applicants. The CCCDC is comprised of the Colbert County Probate Judge as ex officio chair of the committee and three members appointed by our local legislative delegation. Emily Benson, Assistant Chief Probate Clerk, serves as administrator for the commission. The commission meets quarterly. All inquires should be directed to Emily Benson at (256) 314-5820. For further information and regulations, please see the Online Grant Application.
